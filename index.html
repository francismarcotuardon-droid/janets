About Us

Welcome to Janet's Haven

Janet's Haven is a premier boarding house located in the heart of Tacurong City. We provide a safe, comfortable, and welcoming environment for students, professionals, and travelers seeking quality accommodation.

Our modern facilities are designed with your comfort in mind, featuring spacious rooms, clean common areas, and all the amenities you need for a pleasant stay.

Rules & Regulations

  • Respect other boarders' privacy and personal space
  • Maintain cleanliness in all common areas
  • Observe quiet hours from 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM
  • No smoking inside the premises
  • Guests must register at the front desk
